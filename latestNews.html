Latest News: Headwaters Project Funding!

New Donations!


In August of 2022, a $25,000 grant from the Aldarra Foundation, was secured by the Arboretum Foundation. In September, this was matched by a second $25,000 grant from the Chabot Family Fund! These critical early commitments, to restoring the headwaters of Arboretum Creek, will undoubtably inspire additional construction funding!



30% Design Completed:


As of July 2022 the engineering team finished the final 30% Design Report for our Headwaters Project. The report contains improved design documents for the required piping, water treatment, and an updated construction estimate. To learn more select one of the links below.

60% Design - Starting Soon:


A new design contract has been signed with Jacobs Engineering and we are scheduled to reach the 60% design level in 2023. The design should be totally finshed in 2024. We hope for Construction to begin in 2025. Our current King County funding should cover the design investment. Click Here to help with construction funding.
